Privacy Policy

What we collect: Twitch opaque IDs, (optionally) Twitch usernames if you authorize, item purchases/redemptions, and StreamElements points totals if you opt in.

Why: To show your inventory/coins, process redemptions, prevent abuse, and display recent redemptions on stream.

Sharing: No sale or sharing with third parties. Data is used only to operate this Extension.

Retention: Inventory and redemption logs are retained as needed for functionality and troubleshooting.

Your choices: You can stop using the Extension at any time. You may request deletion of redemption/inventory records via the contact below.
